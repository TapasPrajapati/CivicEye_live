Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I file a new FIR?

To file a new FIR, navigate to the FIR Management page and click the "New FIR" button. Fill in all required information including incident type, location, and description.

How can I track case status?

Case status can be tracked from the FIR Management page. Use the search function to find specific cases or filter by status to view all cases in a particular state.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password from the Admin Profile page. Navigate to the "Change Password" section and follow the prompts to update your credentials.

How do I generate reports?

Reports can be generated from the Analytics & Reports page. Select your date range and click "Generate Report". You can export reports in CSV, PDF, or Excel format.
