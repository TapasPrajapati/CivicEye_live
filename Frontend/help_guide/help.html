Help Guide

Learn how to use CivicEye effectively and get answers to common questions

How to Report a Case

  • Click on the "Report Case" button in the navigation menu
  • Fill in all required details about the incident
  • Upload any relevant photos or documents
  • Review your information and submit the report
  • Save the case tracking number for future reference

Tracking Your Case

  • Go to the "Track Case" section
  • Enter your case tracking number
  • View the current status and updates
  • Check investigator comments and next steps
  • Contact support if you need additional information
