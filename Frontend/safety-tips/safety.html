Safety Tips & Guidelines

Essential safety measures to protect yourself and your loved ones

Home Safety

  • Install proper locks on all doors and windows
  • Keep emergency numbers readily available
  • Install security cameras and motion sensors
  • Don't share house keys with strangers
  • Keep valuables in a secure safe

Digital Safety

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Be cautious with personal information online
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links
  • Keep software and apps updated

Personal Safety

  • Stay aware of your surroundings
  • Share location with trusted contacts
  • Avoid walking alone at night
  • Keep emergency contacts on speed dial
  • Trust your instincts

Travel Safety

  • Use verified transportation services
  • Share trip details with family
  • Keep valuables secure while traveling
  • Be cautious in unfamiliar areas
  • Keep emergency cash handy

Emergency Preparedness

  • Keep a first-aid kit at home
  • Know emergency exits in buildings
  • Have an emergency evacuation plan
  • Store important documents safely
  • Keep emergency supplies ready

Online Safety

  • Use secure Wi-Fi networks
  • Be careful with online transactions
  • Don't share OTP with anyone
  • Report suspicious activities
  • Use antivirus software
